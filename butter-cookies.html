Butter Cookies

Servings: 24 | Calories: 80 | Carbs: 7g

Dry Ingredients

  • 2 c almond flour
  • 2 tbsp tapioca flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 c white sugar

Wet Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Mix flour, baking soda & salt in a small bowl
  2. Mix sugars and wet ingredients in a medium bowl
  3. Use a spatula to mix dry ingredients with wet
  4. Scoop dough balls onto baking sheet or muffin top pan
  5. Bake @ 350Â° for 9 minutes
