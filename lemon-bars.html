Lemon Bars

Servings: 24 | Calories: 130 | Carbs: 22g

Dry Ingredients

  • 3/4 c + 2 1/2 tbsp oat flour
  • 1/2 c + 3 tbsp sorghum flour [crust]
  • 1/4 c sorghum flour [filling]
  • 1 c rolled oats
  • 1/2 c coconut sugar
  • 1 c white sugar
  • 1 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Wet Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2/3 c lemon juice
  • 8 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â° and line a 9x9 metal baking pan with parchment paper
  2. Mix flour, oats, sugar, baking powder, salt & cinnamon in a medium bowl
  3. Mix melted butter, maple syrup & vanilla with a spatula until evenly combined
  4. Place mixture in baking pan and spread evenly
  5. Bake 10 minutes, then let cool for 10 minutes
  6. Mix eggs, lemon juice, white sugar & sorghum flour in a medium bowl with a hand mixer
  7. Spread filling evenly over baked crust
  8. Bake for 25 minutes
  9. Cool before slicing and frost with powdered sugar (optional)
