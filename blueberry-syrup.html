Blueberry Syrup

Servings: 5 (1/3 c) | Calories: 110 | Carbs: 28g

Dry Ingredients

  • 1/2 c white sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Wet Ingredients

  • 1/2 c water
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 c blueberries

Steps

  1. Bring water, lemon juice, sugar and cornstarch to a boil
  2. Add blueberries and return to a boil
  3. Let cool and serve
