Oat Flour Muffins

Servings: 12 | Calories: 215 | Carbs: 26g

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 c oat flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 c white sugar

Wet Ingredients

  • 1/2 c coconut oil melted
  • 1 c plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a small bowl
  2. Mix wet ingredients in a medium bowl
  3. Use a spatula to mix dry ingredients with wet
  4. Scoop batter into a lined muffin pan
  5. Add fun ingredients like blueberries or chocolate chips (optional)
  6. Bake @ 350Â° for 24 minutes
  7. Eat muffin to ensure tastiness

Note: fun ingredients add calories and carbs!
