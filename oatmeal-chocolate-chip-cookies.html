Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

Servings: 18 | Calories: 160 | Carbs: 18g

Dry Ingredients

  • 3/4 c rolled oats
  • 1/4 c almond flour
  • 1/2 c oat flour
  • 2 tbsp potato flour
  • 2 tbsp coconut flour
  • 6 tbsp chocolate chips
  • 3/4 c brown sugar
  • 1/4 c white sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Wet Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Mix oats, flours, baking soda & salt in a small bowl
  2. Mix sugars in a medium bowl
  3. Melt/whisk butter on stove until frothy
  4. Mix butter with sugar and add egg, milk & vanilla
  5. Use a spatula to mix dry ingredients with wet
  6. Add in chocolate chips
  7. Place in freezer while preheating oven to 325Â°
  8. Scoop dough balls onto baking sheet or muffin top pan
  9. Bake for 7 minutes
